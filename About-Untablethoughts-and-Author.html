About UnstableThoughts

UnstableThoughts is an independent writing platform focused on political philosophy, Indian politics, development, and social issues. It values slow thinking, honest doubt, and perspectives that evolve rather than remain fixed.

Because ideas should never become inflexible. Here, instability represents intellectual honesty, openness, and continuous growth.

The name says it all. "Unstable" is not a flaw — it is a feature. Fixed ideas are fragile. Ideas that can be questioned, challenged, and rebuilt are the ones worth holding.

The Author

Hi, I am Abin.

On this website, I explore insights into political philosophy, Indian politics, development, and social issues.

Read with an open mind and form your own understanding of the truth. There are many things in our country that need to change in order for us to grow.

I am an Indian who wants to see India become a developed nation and a better place to live.

I assure you that the writing here is not biased toward any individual, political party, or organization.

The goal is simple: to create a meaningful impact through honest and thoughtful writing.
