About This Platform


UnstableThoughts is an independent writing platform focused on political philosophy, Indian politics, development, and social issues.


It values slow thinking, honest doubt, and perspectives that evolve rather than remain fixed.


Why “Unstable”?

Because ideas should never become inflexible. Here, instability represents intellectual honesty, openness, and continuous growth.


About the Author

Hi, I am Abin.

Read with an open mind and form your own understanding of the truth.
